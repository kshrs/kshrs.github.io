The start of a new habit - blogging

- by Kishor 09 Dec,2025

Blogging has always fascinated me since my childhood, but I have never really taken it seriously. I grew up using GNU/Linux and reading HowTo blogs, GitHub READMEs and dev posts have been a part of me.

But now, the purpose of this blogging journey is to document my ML Research journey and general learnings. Inspired by @neuralpat(X.com), @BorisMeinardus(X.com, YouTube), I have decided to test whether writing blogs actually helps the overall learning and understanding process. [ actually his youtube video added confidence to my idea of starting a blog Video: Why Everyone Studying AI Should Create Content ]

My blogs will be solely focused on three main things(but not limited to):

  • To document my overall learning and journey in life.
  • To learn ML Research Concepts with more clarity by writing about it, as if teaching someone. The Feynman Technique
  • To improve my focus on learning and being accountable in the overall journey. The Grind Mindset

As the ML Research part has more value/importance, I am gonna focus more on that.

Right now, I have decided to focus more on pursuing a ML Research Career than to focus on general academics. No matter what stops me(academics).

Hope this blog post stays as a reminder to focus on my journey and not on the distractions.

The grind culture made me understand that I have stayed with the wrong group of people and actually wasted a year doing nothing. So time to fix this shit rn.

So gotta go and start working.
